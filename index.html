Privacy Policy – LocaVN

LocaVN is committed to protecting the privacy and personal data of users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: such as email and name when users log in
  • Location Data (GPS): used to show nearby places when permission is granted
  • Images: only when users voluntarily capture and submit photos for reports or feedback
  • Usage Data: to improve app performance and user experience

2. How We Use Information

The collected data is used to:

  • Provide map services and location-based search
  • Allow users to submit reports and feedback
  • Improve application functionality and performance
  • Support system management and analytics

3. Camera Permission

The app only accesses the camera when the user actively chooses to take a photo. No images are captured without user consent.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share personal data with third parties, except when required by law or authorized government agencies.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect user data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or loss.

6. User Rights

Users have the right to:

  • Access and update their personal information
  • Request deletion of their account and related data

7. Account Deletion

Users can request account deletion directly within the app or by contacting our support email. We will process deletion requests within a reasonable timeframe in accordance with applicable laws.

8. Children's Privacy

This app is intended for general users and does not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
